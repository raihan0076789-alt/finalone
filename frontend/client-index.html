Client Journey

From Idea to Dream Home
in 4 Simple Steps

01

Create Your Free Client Account

Sign up in seconds. Tell us about your project — residential, commercial, renovation — your budget, location, and style preferences. No architect account needed.

02

Discover & Connect with Architects

Browse our verified architect directory. View their 3D portfolios, read client reviews, check availability and rates. Send a direct project inquiry with one click.

03

Collaborate on Your Design

Work together on your floor plan using SmartArch's AI tools. Provide feedback on 2D blueprints and 3D models. Request changes in real-time from your client dashboard.

Monitor construction progress, approve milestone completions, and manage payments — all from your secure client portal. Your dream home, built with confidence.
