Section 02

Service Description

The SmartArch Client Portal is a web-based platform that connects homeowners, property developers, and construction clients with verified professional architects. The portal provides the following core services:

  • Architect Discovery: Browse verified architect profiles including their specialization, experience, location, portfolio, and ratings to find the right match for your project.
  • Project Brief Management: Create, edit, and manage detailed project briefs describing your construction or design requirements, including budget, timeline, land size, style preferences, and attachments.
  • Connection Requests: Send connection requests directly to architects of your choice, with an introductory message and optional project brief context.
  • In-Portal Messaging: Once an architect accepts your connection, communicate directly through the built-in chat system including text and image messages.
  • File Attachments: Upload reference images, site plans, and documents to your project briefs to provide architects with comprehensive project context.
  • Support Ticketing: Submit and track support requests through the integrated ticket system if you need assistance.

We reserve the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue any feature or service component at any time with reasonable notice where practicable.
