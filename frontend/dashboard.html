How to Use SmartArch
Follow these steps to create and visualize your architecture project
Phase 1 — Create Your Project
1
Go to My Projects
From the sidebar, click My Projects. Then click the + New Project button to begin.
2
Name Your Project
Enter a project name and optionally pick a template. Templates give you a head start with a pre-built room layout.
3
Open the Architect Tool
Once created, click Open on your project card. This launches the full design canvas.
Phase 2 — Build Your 2D Floor Plan
4
Add Rooms
Use the left panel to drag rooms (bedroom, kitchen, bathroom, etc.) onto the canvas. Resize them by dragging the edges.
5
Place Doors & Windows
Select a wall on any room, then use the toolbar to add a door or window. Click to position it along the wall.
6
Add Staircases & Multiple Floors
Use the floor panel at the bottom to add a new floor. Drag a staircase element to connect floors in your plan.
7
Label & Save
Double-click any room to rename it. Press Ctrl + S or click Save in the toolbar to save your progress.
Phase 3 — View & Export in 3D
8
Switch to 3D View
Click the 3D View toggle in the top toolbar. Your 2D plan is automatically converted to a 3D model.
9
Navigate the 3D Scene
Left click + drag to orbit. Scroll to zoom. Right click + drag to pan around your model.
10
Export Your Model
Click Export in the toolbar to download your design as CAD, OBJ, STL, or GLTF for use in other tools.
Tip: Use the AI Chat in the architect tool to ask questions like "suggest a 3-bedroom layout" or "how do I add a garage?" — powered by your local AI model.
