About the Client Portal

Your Gateway to
World-Class Architects

SmartArch's Client Portal bridges the gap between homeowners, developers, and professional architects. Whether you're planning your dream home, a commercial space, or a renovation — our platform gives you everything you need to find the right architect, share your vision, and manage your project from concept to completion.

We believe great architecture should be accessible to everyone. By combining AI-powered tools with a curated network of verified professionals, we make the journey from idea to blueprint seamless, transparent, and stress-free.

Platform Overview

What is the SmartArch Client Portal?

The Client Portal is a dedicated space designed specifically for people who want to build, renovate, or design spaces. It connects you directly to a curated network of professional architects — letting you browse profiles, review portfolios, send connection requests, and communicate in real time.

Once connected with an architect, you can share your project brief, upload reference materials, exchange messages, and track progress — all in one secure, easy-to-use platform backed by a robust Node.js + MongoDB infrastructure.

Find Verified Architects

Browse a curated directory of professional architects. Filter by specialization, experience, rating, and location to find your perfect match.

Direct Communication

Send connection requests with personalised intro messages. Once accepted, chat directly with your architect — text and image sharing included.

Project Brief Management

Create detailed project briefs covering budget, land size, room requirements, style preferences, and timeline. Attach reference images and documents.

Secure & Private

Your project data is protected by JWT authentication, bcrypt password hashing, and role-based access control — only you and your connected architect can see your chat.

Client Journey

From Idea to Architect in Minutes

The SmartArch Client Portal is designed to get you from concept to connected architect as smoothly as possible.

01
Create Your Client Account

Register with your name, email, and password. You'll receive an OTP to verify your email — then you're ready to explore.

02
Build Your Project Brief

Describe your project — type, budget, land size, style, rooms, and timeline. Upload reference images or floor plans. Save as draft or publish immediately.

03
Browse & Filter Architects

Use the Find Architects view to filter by specialization, experience, rating, and location. View full profiles with portfolios and past projects.

04
Send a Connection Request

Click Connect on any architect's profile. Write a personalised intro message and optionally attach one of your project briefs. The architect will be notified immediately.

05
Chat & Collaborate

Once the architect accepts your request, the chat unlocks. Exchange text messages, share reference images, and discuss your vision in real time.

06
Move to Design

Your architect works within their SmartArch dashboard — generating AI floor plans, creating 3D models, and sharing progress directly back to you through the platform.

Full Workflow

Your Complete Project Lifecycle

From account creation to a finished architectural design — here's exactly what happens at each stage of the SmartArch client journey.

01
Registration & Email Verification
Register with your name, email, and password. A 6-digit OTP is emailed immediately. Verify your account (up to 5 resends per day allowed). Google OAuth is also supported for one-click sign-in.
02
Create Your First Project Brief
Go to "My Projects" in your dashboard and click New Project. Fill in the 3-step wizard: (1) basic info and type, (2) budget, land size, style, rooms, and timeline, (3) attach reference files. Submit to save as Active.
03
Discover the Right Architect
Use the Find Architects section to search and filter the architect directory. Click any card to open a full profile modal showing their bio, specialization, experience, rating, portfolio links, and public projects.
04
Send a Connection Request
Click Connect on any architect profile. Write a personalised introduction and optionally attach one of your project briefs. The architect sees an unread badge in their dashboard and can accept or reject your request.
05
Chat & Share Your Vision
Once the architect accepts, the Chat button activates. Exchange messages, share inspiration images, clarify requirements, and discuss design directions. The architect can view your full project brief directly from their dashboard.
06
Architect Designs Your Space
Your architect uses SmartArch's AI-powered design tools — generating floor plans with BSP algorithms, creating 3D models, running AI feedback analysis, and exporting designs in OBJ, GLTF, CAD, and PDF formats.
